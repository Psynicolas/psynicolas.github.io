Design within constraints sparks innovation. Working with Google's free toolkit forced modular thinking, clarity, and creative structuring that ended up being pedagogical advantages, not limitations. In a fast-growing EdTech delivering live and recorded programs across multiple verticals, the post-session survey was the primary mechanism for capturing the student voice. The instrument had grown organically across teams, with items added over time and no central authority enforcing measurement integrity. As Head of Learning, I made this audit a P0 priority. The four findingsLos cuatro hallazgos

A systematic review of every item against validity and reliability criteria surfaced four distinct threats compromising the instrument's ability to capture what it claimed to measure.Una revisión sistemática de cada ítem contra criterios de validez y confiabilidad reveló cuatro amenazas distintas que comprometían la capacidad del instrumento de capturar lo que decía medir.

FINDING 01

Order biasSesgo de orden

Items appeared in a fixed sequence that primed respondents toward earlier topics. Late-position items received systematically lower engagement, distorting comparative metrics across constructs.Los ítems aparecían en secuencia fija que cebaba a los respondentes hacia temas tempranos. Los ítems en posición tardía recibían engagement sistemáticamente menor, distorsionando las métricas comparativas entre constructos.

Threat: scale comparabilityAmenaza: comparabilidad de escala
FINDING 02

Double negationDoble negación

Several items contained negatively-worded statements paired with agreement scales, producing cognitive load that introduced response noise. Respondents agreed with statements that contradicted their actual intent.Varios ítems contenían formulaciones negadas en combinación con escalas de acuerdo, produciendo carga cognitiva que introducía ruido. Los respondentes acordaban con afirmaciones que contradecían su intención real.

Threat: response validityAmenaza: validez de respuesta
FINDING 03

Scale asymmetryAsimetría de escalas

Different items used different response scales (1-5, 1-7, 1-10) within the same survey. Averaging across them as if they were comparable produced misleading composite scores.Distintos ítems usaban distintas escalas (1-5, 1-7, 1-10) dentro de la misma encuesta. Promediar a través de ellos como si fueran comparables producía puntajes compuestos engañosos.

Threat: aggregation integrityAmenaza: integridad de agregación
FINDING 04

Construct validityValidez de constructo

Items intended to measure distinct constructs (facilitator quality, content relevance, perceived learning) were operationally entangled — a single item conflated multiple dimensions, making it impossible to attribute variance to a specific source.Ítems pensados para medir constructos distintos (calidad del formador, relevancia del contenido, aprendizaje percibido) estaban operacionalmente entrelazados — un solo ítem confundía múltiples dimensiones, haciendo imposible atribuir varianza a una fuente específica.

Threat: causal interpretationAmenaza: interpretación causal
The redesign — triangulation architectureEl rediseño — arquitectura de triangulación

Rather than patching items one by one, I proposed a structural redesign anchored in triangulation across three independent signals: a standardized north-star metric (NPS), construct-specific items grouped in coherent blocks, and open-ended qualitative comments analyzed thematically. Each signal compensates the weaknesses of the others. En lugar de parchar ítems uno por uno, propuse un rediseño estructural anclado en triangulación de tres señales independientes: una métrica north-star estandarizada (NPS), ítems específicos por constructo agrupados en bloques coherentes, y comentarios abiertos cualitativos analizados temáticamente. Cada señal compensa las debilidades de las otras.

What I learnedQué aprendí

Data governance is a learning-design problem disguised as an ops problem. If you don't agree on what learning is, you can't agree on how to measure it, and you can't make institutional decisions about it. Centralizing the conversation at the construct-definition layer — not at the dashboard layer — was the move that unlocked alignment. NPS is necessary but not sufficient. It's a coordination metric across teams, but it cannot substitute construct-specific indicators that actually inform pedagogical iteration. Methodological rigor is a leadership skill. The Head of Learning who can name a validity threat changes how the whole organization treats its data. La gobernanza de datos es un problema de diseño instruccional disfrazado de problema de ops. Si no acuerdan qué es el aprendizaje, no pueden acordar cómo medirlo, y no pueden tomar decisiones institucionales al respecto. Centralizar la conversación en la capa de definición de constructos — no en la del dashboard — fue lo que destrabó el alineamiento. NPS es necesario pero no suficiente. Es una métrica de coordinación entre equipos, pero no puede sustituir los indicadores específicos de constructo que realmente informan la iteración pedagógica. El rigor metodológico es una habilidad de liderazgo. El Head of Learning que puede nombrar una amenaza de validez cambia cómo toda la organización trata sus datos.

El sistema fue diseñado para detectar esto; el pool funcionó como funnel de talento para roles adyacentes, no solo como banca de docencia.

OutcomeResultado

The pool grew from 4 internal facilitators in year one to 12 hybrid facilitators by year five, several of whom worked across multiple Caserta programs. Four facilitators stayed for the entire five-year period, forming the pedagogical core. Rotation was real (~8 transitions over five years) but never destabilized delivery thanks to the always-on recruitment channel and the two-week onboarding standard. The system sustained 10 consecutive iterations of Profes en Red and enabled parallel program launches that would have been impossible with the original 4-person team. What I learnedQué aprendí

A facilitator pool is a system, not a list. Quality at scale comes from the connective tissue — rubric, coaching, weekly ritual — not from individual hires. Personalize the development, not the standards. The Enneagram-based communicator profiling let me adapt the coaching for each person while keeping the institutional bar constant. Recruitment never stops. A 30-candidate-deep pipeline is not paranoia; it's what lets you say yes to new programs without lowering the quality of existing ones.
